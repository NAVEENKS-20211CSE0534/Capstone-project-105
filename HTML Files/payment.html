Overview of Payment Methods

We offer a variety of payment methods to ensure a seamless shopping experience. Whether you prefer paying with cash at the time of delivery or using online payment gateways, we've got you covered. Hereâ€™s a look at the available options:

1. Cash on Delivery (COD)

Cash on Delivery is a payment option that allows you to pay for your order in cash at the time of delivery. This method is convenient and ensures that you only pay when your goods arrive at your doorstep.

  • No need to pay upfront.
  • Enjoy the convenience of inspecting your purchase before payment.
  • A widely accepted method preferred by many customers.
  • Available for select items and regions.
2. Online Payment via Razorpay

We support secure online payments through Razorpay, one of India's leading payment gateways. You can choose from a variety of payment options:

  • Google Pay: Use Google Pay for fast and secure UPI payments directly from your bank account.
  • PhonePe: Make instant payments using PhonePe, a popular digital wallet and UPI platform.
  • Credit/Debit Card: Pay using your Visa, MasterCard, or any other major credit/debit card. Just enter your card details for a quick checkout.
  • UPI ID: You can pay using your UPI ID through various banking apps for a quick transaction without needing to enter your card details.
  • Wallet Payments: Payments can also be done through various wallets like Paytm and others linked with Razorpay.
3. Black Electronic Payment (Black Elc)

This payment method refers to using electronic payment through dedicated platforms, particularly for bulk orders or large-item purchases. It allows for more streamlined payment processing when making significant purchases.

  • Offers a secure and efficient transaction process.
  • Suitable for businesses or personal use for big orders.
  • Integration with multiple financial institutions for easy transactions.
